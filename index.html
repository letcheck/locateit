What's Locate it?.

Locateit is web application where users all around the world can upload media(video, text, picture) and geotag it! But why people will do that? The answer is simple for social purpose and achievement! Because other users can rate your media and so only the best will appear first . They can of course see all the media put by the other and they can follow a location to receive update when something is post there!
